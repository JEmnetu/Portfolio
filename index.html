My name is Jacob Habtemariam, and I am a native of Owings Mills, Maryland. I graduated from Franklin Highschool in Reisterstown MD in 2013, and from the University of Maryland, College Park in 2017. I am currently enrolled in the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at Johns Hopkins.

While I was born and raised in Owings Mills, I am also a first generation American. My parents and my older sister were all born in Eritrea, a small nation in the Horn of Africa that was once an Italian colony as well as an Ethiopian province. I speak Tigrinya, the national language of Eritrea. I also play the Kirar, a traditional strung instrument from Eritrea.
